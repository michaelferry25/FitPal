Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

1. Introduction

At FitPal, we are committed to protecting and respecting your privacy in accordance with GDPR and Ireland's Data Protection Act 2018.

2. Information We Collect

We use your data to provide personalized services, track progress, send notifications, and improve FitPal. We never sell your data to third parties.

4. Data Storage

Data is securely stored on encrypted servers located in Ireland. Access is restricted to authorized personnel only.

5. Sharing Your Information

We may share data with trusted third-party providers for service functionality (e.g., cloud hosting). All partners comply with GDPR regulations.

6. Your Rights
